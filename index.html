Biography

I am currently a research assistant professor at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (HK PolyU). Prior to this, I worked as a research associate with Prof. Ming Liu, at the Robotics Institute, Department of Electronic and Computer Engineering, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I received the Ph.D degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) under the supervision of Prof. Max Qing-Hu Meng in 2017, the master's degree from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2012, and the bachelor's degree from Hefei University of Technology (HFUT) in 2009. My first-author Robotics and Autonomous Systems (RAS) papers published in 2017 and 2018 respectively ranked No.1 and No.2 in the most cited articles of recent three years in this journal. My first-author IEEE Robotics and Automation Letters (RAL) paper published in 2019 was identified as one of the popular articles in this journal. I received the Best Paper award at the IEEE-ROBIO 2019 conference, and the Best Student Paper Finalist award at the IEEE-ROBIO 2015 conference, both as the first author. I am the PI of an NSFC Young Scientists Fund project, and have participated in many Hong Kong and China Mainland projects, such as RGC-CRF, ITF, NSFC, etc. I am a lead guest editor for Frontiers in Robotics and AI. I am a reviewer for many top-tier journals and conferences, such as TRO, IJCV, RAL, TASE, TITS, TNNLS, TCSVT, TMM, PR, ICRA, IROS, etc.

Research Interests

Autonomous Driving, Robotics and Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning, Robotic Perception, Autonomous Navigation, Localization and Mapping, Mobile Robots, Unmanned Systems, Advanced Driver Assistance Systems, Semantic Scene Understanding, Reinforcement Learning, Imitation Learning, End-to-End Control, Trajectory Planning and Prediction, etc.
